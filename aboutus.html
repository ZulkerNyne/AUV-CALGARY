About Us

AUV Calgary is a newly formed, student-led underwater robotics team focused on designing and building Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs) for real-world challenges and international competition.

We bring together students from various disciplines — mechanical engineering, electrical systems, embedded software, AI, and business — to build a system capable of intelligent underwater navigation, object detection, and mission execution.

While our journey has just begun, our vision is clear: to push the boundaries of autonomy and innovation beneath the surface. We’re learning fast, building smart, and growing together as a close-knit team dedicated to exploration and excellence.
