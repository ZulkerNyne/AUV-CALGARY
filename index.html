Beneath the Surface

We are a student-led underwater robotics team, designing and building Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs) to tackle complex, real-world challenges below the surface. Our mission is to blend innovative engineering with cutting-edge software — all while fostering a tight-knit, interdisciplinary team.

From mechanical design and electrical systems to embedded control and autonomy, our members dive into every stage of the engineering lifecycle. Whether we’re prototyping a new propulsion system or writing path-planning algorithms, every component is student-built, mission-driven, and competition-focused.
