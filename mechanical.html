Mechanical Subteam

The mechanical subteam uses Solidworks to design the custom enclosures, racks, and structures on the sub. Throughout the design process, rigorous finite element analysis is run on each assembly to ensure every part will perform successfully under high loads. Every component is manufactured in-house with mills, lathes, and CNC machines to a tolerance of 0.005 inches. Mechanical members then integrate and test all assemblies up to fifty feet underwater.
