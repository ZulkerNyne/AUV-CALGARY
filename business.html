Business Subteam

The business subteam manages all external-facing aspects of the team — from securing sponsorships and funding to branding, outreach, and events. Members develop strategic partnerships with industry leaders, prepare sponsorship packages, and maintain relationships with donors and collaborators. They also handle social media, design marketing materials, and organize recruitment campaigns to grow campus presence. Every opportunity to showcase the team — online or in-person — is led by the business subteam’s creative and strategic direction.
